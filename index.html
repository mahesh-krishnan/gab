What is Global Azure Bootcamp?

On, Saturday, April 25, 2015  we are out to set some records again!                      

In April of 2013 the first Global Windows Azure Bootcamp  was held at more than 90 locations around the globe! In March 2014 it was topped with 136 locations!  This year we are again doing a one day deep dive class to help thousands of people get up to speed on developing Cloud Computing Applications for Azure .

In addition to this great learning opportunity we will have another set of hands on labs

MEET OUR SPEAKERS




James Bannan

James, Microsoft MVP for Enterprise Client Management


David O'Brien

David, Microsoft MVP for System Center Cloud and Datacenter Management 


Orin Thomas

Orin Thomas is an MVP, an MCT and has a string of Microsoft MCSE and MCITP certifications. He has written more than 30 books for Microsoft Press and is a contributing editor at Windows IT Pro magazine.


Chris Padgett

Chris Padgett has been implementing solutions using Microsoft's latest technologies since 2001, including one of the first .NET solutions in Australia. He is passionate about the API-first, hybrid, and security approaches to cloud computing, especially how these approaches can be used to meet today's IT challenges. He is a regular speaker at events, including Tech Ed Australia. He has also been co-organising the Azure special interest group in Melbourne since 2011


Mitch Denny

Mitch Denny is a Microsoft MVP (ALM), ASP Insider and the Chief Technology Officer of Readify. Mitch spends his time working with all kinds of organisations around their technology strategy and is particularly interested in cloud computing, mobility, ALM and DevOps and emerging trends such as the Internet-of-Things


Tao Yang

Tao is a  Microsoft MVP for System Center Cloud and Datcenter Management.  He is a senior System Center specialist with 15 years of experience in IT. Although Tao's skill set spans across multiple System Center products, his primary focus is Operations Manager and MP development.


Mahesh Krishnan

Mahesh works at Readify in Melbourne. He is an author, speaker and Microsoft Azure MVP. He is also active in the .NET community in Melbourne, runs the Azure Meetup and organises the conference DDD Melbourne MVP


Dan Kregor

Dan Kregor is a Microsoft System Center Cloud and Datacenter Management MVP and Managing Principal Consultant at a private consulting firm in Melbourne, Australia. Dan is the founder, producer and host of the Inside Podcast Network (Inside Central, WAP Wrap and SkillingUp), a series of podcasts dedicated to System Center and the enterprise IT professional. 


John Azariah

John Azariah has been writing software for over two decades and has worked in several companies in the US, India, Singapore, and Australia. He has spoken at several conferences including NDC Oslo, NDC London, TechEd AU, TechEd NZ and DDD Melbourne, and actively participates in the .NET, Azure and F# communities. He is an Azure MVP and a functional programming enthusiast


Daniel Mar

Daniel is a Principal Consultant with Infront Consulting Group in Australia. He was Singapore’s first Microsoft Most Valuable Professional for Cloud and Datacenter Management as well as also being a Citrix Desktop MVP but now spends his time in Melbourne.  


Yaniv Rodesnki

Yaniv currently works at UXC Professional Solutions as Principal Consultant. He is an author, speaker, developer and also an Azure MVP


Bill Chesnut

Bill Chesnut is a Microsoft Integration MVP and Head of Technology for Mexia Consulting. Bill has been involved with .Net, BizTalk Server and Azure since the release of each, including strong communities involvement in user groups around these technologies
